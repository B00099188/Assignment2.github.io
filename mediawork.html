Media Work

Stop-Motion Project

For my first semester Media Work we were assigned work to create a stopmotion video for YouTube. The only requirements was that the video had to be 2 minutes in length and had to be copyright friendly. I used Lego for stop-motion video because of the animation quality of the individual parts. I named it "Lego iconsistency" to make light of the obvious inconsistency between shots.

Christmas Designs

For my second semester design work we were assigned work to create a Video on a concept we have been creating for the entire semester, I shot and edited the video over 2 weeks which is called "The Saga of Daniel O'Donnell". I used pins and sticks to move the props to simulate a puppet show. The password for the Video is CDMStorytelling.
